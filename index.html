Lavender

A community based stock trading platform built using Ruby on Rails.

  • Create or join groups for community based investing.
  • Using APIs such as Plaid, Stock Quote, and Stripe, you are able to buy and sell stocks as well as connect to your bank account.
  • Each group is divvied up into shares based on the algorithm for how much each member has invested.
  • Create a voting poll that needs at least 51% to execute each trade.
